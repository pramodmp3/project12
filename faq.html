How does your Analytics product handle large datasets?

▼

Our Analytics platform is built on a scalable, distributed architecture designed to process and analyze petabytes of data in real-time. We utilize high-performance computing clusters to ensure low-latency insights, regardless of volume.

Can I integrate the CRM with my existing email provider?

▼

Yes, our CRM features seamless integration with all major email services (Gmail, Outlook 365, etc.) via secure API connections, allowing you to sync contacts, track correspondence, and manage leads directly from your inbox.

What is Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and how does DevOps Pro use it?

▼

IaC is the practice of managing and provisioning infrastructure through code instead of manual processes. DevOps Pro uses technologies like Terraform and Ansible to ensure your environments are consistent, repeatable, and version-controlled, drastically reducing deployment errors.

What payment methods do you accept for subscriptions?

▼

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and wire transfers for annual enterprise plans. You can update your billing details anytime in your account settings.

How do I contact customer support?

▼

You can reach our dedicated support team 24/7 via live chat directly within the app, or by submitting a ticket through the "Contact Us" page on our website. Priority support is available for Pro and Enterprise subscribers.
